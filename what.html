logistics

and frequently asked questions

travel

The nearest airport is St. Louis Lambert International Airport (STL). If you're traveling from Boston, Southwest is the only airline that will fly you there directly. All of the weekend's activities will take place in Washington, MO, which is about an hour's drive from the airport. There is no public transportation between the two, so we recommend getting a rental car.

attire

We request formal attire for Saturday’s events. Jackets/ties and long dresses would be appropriate.

field day

The extended wedding party is invited to arrive a day early for lawn games, drinking games, and team competitions. This event is not suitable for children. Athletic attire recommended.
