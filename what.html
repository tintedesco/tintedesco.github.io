and frequently asked questions

are you married?

Technically, yes. We had a small ceremony with a virtual audience (but real paperwork) last year. This year we'll be doing a renewal of vows.

what about covid?

We are vaccinated! And we hope you are too. We have high-risk family members who will be attending, and we ask that you respect their safety by protecting yourself.

travel

The nearest airport is St. Louis Lambert International Airport (STL). All of the wedding activities will be in the city of Washington, which is about an hour's drive from the airport. There is no public transportation between the two. We recommend getting a rental car. (Free parking is available at all of the wedding locations.)

attire

We request formal attire for the wedding day. Long dresses and jackets with ties would be appropriate.

children

We respectfully request that little ones stay home. We love your kids! (Stephanie loves the human ones and Luke loves the four-legged ones.) But we think it's in their best interests to sit this one out.

field day

The extended wedding party is invited to arrive a day early for lawn games, drinking games, and sporting competitions. This event is not suitable for children. If you'll be in town and would like to join us, please let us know!
